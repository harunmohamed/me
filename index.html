President's Award Club Kenya

The President's Award Kenya is a club in Kenya where students get to portray character excellence by involving themselves in high resilience activities such as extreme hiking and camping in the bronze and silver levels. The Gold level entails students take up a community enrichment project where they give back to society. For my gold level project, I and my group went to the Huruma Children's Home in Kenya and built for them a biogas digester, repainted the school, and donated books to the school. We also got to spend time with the children and it was a really eye-opening experience. I thereafter received the Gold Accolade from the President of Kenya Uhuru Kenyatta at a State House Function in Kenya
